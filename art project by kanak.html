Madhubani Painting

Madhubani painting, originating from Bihar, is known for its intricate designs and vibrant colors. Artists use fingers, twigs, brushes, and matchsticks to create these geometric and symbolic designs.

Warli Painting

Warli painting is a tribal art form from Maharashtra. It depicts daily life scenes, animals, and festivals using simple geometric shapes like circles, triangles, and squares.

Kalamkari Painting

Kalamkari is a hand-painted or block-printed textile art from Andhra Pradesh. This art form often illustrates mythological stories and uses natural dyes for coloring.

Gond Painting

Gond painting is a tribal art from Madhya Pradesh. It features detailed patterns and bright colors to depict nature, animals, and folklore in a distinctive style.

Miniature Painting

Miniature paintings are small, detailed artworks that date back to the Mughal era. They often depict historical events, royal life, and stories from Indian epics like the Ramayana and Mahabharata.
